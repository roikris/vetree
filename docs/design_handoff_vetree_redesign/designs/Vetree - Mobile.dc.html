Pimobendan in Preclinical Myxomatous Mitral Valve Disease

Boswood A, Häggström J, et al. · J Vet Intern Med · 2026
Clinical bottom line

Pimobendan delayed the onset of congestive heart failure in preclinical MMVD dogs by a median of roughly 15 months versus placebo.

Summary

The EPIC study randomised 360 dogs with preclinical MMVD and cardiomegaly to pimobendan or placebo. The primary endpoint was the onset of left-sided CHF, cardiac death, or euthanasia.

Median time to primary endpoint was 1228 days in the pimobendan group versus 766 days with placebo — a clinically meaningful delay that reshaped preclinical treatment guidance.
